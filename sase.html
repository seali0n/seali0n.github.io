GRAPHIC DESIGN

My Role

Over two years, I worked as my org’s publicity intern and officer to create designs that helped in advertising various events, both social and professional. The publications took place over different media platforms as well as print form.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Lightroom

Skills: Graphic Design, Visual Design

Background

UC San Diego Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) is a pre-professional student organization that focuses on preparing members for future career opportunities. The org hosts a variety of professional workshops and events to help develop necessary skills in order to become successful.

One of the biggest events UC San Diego SASE hosts is Dining with Professionals (DWP), a networking event where attendees dine with professionals from industry, research, and academia in the San Diego area. DWP take months to plan and has separate committees corresponding to different needs. I had the experience of working in and eventually leading the Publicity Committee, which is responsible for all publications regarding the event, both online and in print.

The Work
2016-2017 DWP Brochure

The first graphic project that I worked on in my intern year was the Dining with Professionals brochure. Here, I had to introduce the reader to the event and its long-held tradition with UC San Diego SASE, the attending professionals and their work biographies, and the attending companies. I designed the pamphlet to not only inform the reader but also allow readers to use it as notes for any pertinent information.

I made the icons and chose the color palette to create an appealing visual and focused on the technology side of STEM. I chose blue to imitate the many different social medias that use blue in their branding guides.

2017-2018 DWP Sponsorship Package

The following year, I created a sponsorship package to engage with potential sponsors from companies that were interested in attending the event. The goal was to organize all of our information in a clean professional but still impactfulway. I decided to go for a simple formatting and focus on a darker color palette for a more streamlined appearance and red accents for a punch of color.

Fun fact: The hardest part of designing the package was actually the graph. I had to go through several different programs just to make it have the right fit and legible quality for our sponsors. Definitely the most tedious part!

2017-2018 DWP Brochure

The pamphlet was designed to match the sponsorship package in terms of aesthetic. I followed the same layout as last year's design.

For this year’s Dining with Professional event, I wanted to keep the same color scheme as the sponsorship package to adhere to consistency and familiarity.

My Experience

To be able to work with such amazing people has really enriched my college experience. Through SASE, I was able to create graphic design and hone my skill for a purpose that I could stand behind. If it were not for the org’s supportive people who encouraged me, and my mentor who guided me, I would not have had so many great opportunities. Cheesy as it is, I really do think that I found myself in this org and for that, will always have a special place in my heart for its mission and its people.
