TYPOGRAPHY PROJECT

I created varying layouts using the grid system. The goal of the assignment was to see how typography and formatting affected legibility and accessibility. My process started out with rapid sketches to first iterations of digital layouts to critique. Afterwards, I had a critique session and reiterated based on the feedback for the next critique session. After the final session, I refined my creations and submitted my final design.

POSTER DESIGN PROJECT

This project's challenge was creating effective visual communication through use of type, layout, and imagery. For the poster design, I was given a set of text that must be included without any editing or exclusion of words or punctuation. I focused on contrast of colors, fontweight, and spacing of text to increase readability.
